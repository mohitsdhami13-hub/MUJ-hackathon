Where AI ends and the human counselor begins

The AI does not know Mara. It knows her timestamps, her session lengths, the cadence of her returns. It knows that she visited at 3am for eleven consecutive nights after the anniversary. It does not know what that cost her.

The counselor knows both. That is why the console exists — not to replace the counselor's judgment, but to give it more to work with.

Every tapering suggestion, every gentle annotation in this dashboard, passes through a human before it touches Mara. The console has no direct channel to the person grieving. That is a design choice, not a limitation.

Design Constraint #1 — Console v1.0
